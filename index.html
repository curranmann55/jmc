Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tree service lead generation

Why choose TreeLeads Pro? +

As tree service specialists, we understand the unique challenges of your industry. Our proprietary technology targets homeowners specifically looking for tree services, delivering exclusive, high-intent calls directly to your business. We only charge for valid leads, take on all the advertising risk, and provide dedicated account support to maximize your ROI.

How do you generate calls? +

We use sophisticated, AI-powered technology to place targeted ads on major search engines and local directories. Our system specifically targets consumers actively searching for tree services in your area. We take on the cost and risk of paying for ad clicks and impressions, while you only pay for the qualified phone calls we generate.

Do you share my call leads with competitors? +

Absolutely not. All calls we generate are 100% exclusive to your business and are never shared with competitors or other tree service companies. This exclusivity ensures you have the best possible chance to convert each lead into a paying customer.

How many calls should I expect? +

Call volume varies based on your service area, competition level, and the specific tree services you offer. Tree service leads often show seasonal patterns, with higher volumes during storm seasons and spring/fall months. After learning about your business during onboarding, we'll provide estimated call volumes for your specific situation and budget.

Can I pause my campaigns during busy periods? +

Yes! You have complete control to pause and restart your campaigns anytime through your dashboard. Many tree services adjust their lead flow seasonally or when their schedule is fully booked. You can also set campaigns to automatically restart on a specific date.
