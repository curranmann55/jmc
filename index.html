What is a Valid Call?

Valid calls are from people searching for specific tree services you've selected to target. This includes calls from people comparing prices but does not include calls from job seekers, marketing companies, or those inquiring about services outside your selected offerings or service area.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our tree service lead generation.

Why choose TreeLeads Pro?

Operating since 2015, TreeLeads Pro focuses exclusively on generating quality leads for tree service businesses. Using our proprietary AI-based technology, we create and optimize ad campaigns on search engines to generate exclusive inbound calls for your business. We never resell calls or buy them from affiliates. With a dedicated success manager for your account, we work with you to achieve your targeting and ROI goals.

How do you generate calls?

Our sophisticated technology targets consumers actively searching for tree services on major search engines and local directories. We pay for the ad clicks and impressions while only charging you for the phone calls we generate through these ads. This allows you to personally sell your business to interested callers while only paying for qualified leads.

How do the phone calls work?

Once we place ads for your services, you'll begin receiving live phone calls. Callers dial a tracking number which is immediately redirected to your business phone. We don't speak to callers or send email leads. If you don't answer, we allow the caller to leave a voicemail or send you a text message, or we'll send you an audio recording with the service the caller needs.

Do you share my call leads?

No, all calls go directly and exclusively to your business. We never share your leads with competitors or other businesses.

How many calls should I expect?

Call volume varies based on your services, service area, and call price. Seasonal factors also affect tree service call volume. After learning about your business and setting up your campaign, we'll provide an approximate volume estimate. You can adjust your budget at any time to increase or decrease call volume.

How do you determine pricing?

Call prices vary based on the tree services you offer, your geographical area, and local competition. We provide estimated pricing tiers, but will discuss optimal pricing after you create an account. There's no obligation to creating an account ‚Äî it simply gives us information to provide the best price for your campaign.

Can I pause my campaigns temporarily?

Yes! You can pause and restart your campaigns anytime through your dashboard. This is especially helpful during busy periods when you've reached capacity or during weather events that affect tree services. You can also set specific dates for campaigns to restart if needed.

How do I get started?

To get started, create an account on our website. This allows us to determine pricing specific to your service area and service menu. Once your profile is complete, a Customer Success Manager will reach out to discuss pricing and answer any questions. If you have questions about creating an account, give us a call at (844) 555-TREE.
