Understanding Our Pay-Per-Call Model

Transparency is key to our success. Here's exactly what you're paying for.

What is a Valid Lead?

At JMC Agency, we've built our business model on transparency and fairness. You'll only pay for calls that meet all of the following criteria:

  • Homeowner Status: The caller must be a homeowner (not a renter or property manager).
  • Service Intent: The caller must be specifically inquiring about tree removal or tree trimming services.
  • Location Qualification: The caller must be located within your defined service area.
  • Minimum Duration: The call must last at least 60 seconds, ensuring there's a meaningful conversation.
  • First-Time Caller: We only charge for unique callers (within a 30-day period).

Any calls that don't meet these criteria are not billable. This includes wrong numbers, solicitors, non-homeowners, or people looking for services other than tree removal or trimming.

Our Payment Policy

You only pay for valid leads as defined above. Our system automatically tracks and categorizes each call, and you can review all calls in your dashboard. If a call is incorrectly marked as valid, our dispute resolution team will review the recording and credit your account if necessary.
